About the Foundation

SUFAF Foundation, established by Hon. Dr. Sulaiman Abubakar Fana, is a philanthropic organization dedicated to fostering education and skills acquisition within communities. The foundation is driven by a commitment to uplift individuals, particularly those from less privileged backgrounds, by providing them with the tools and opportunities needed to succeed.
